Carbonara in under 10minutes!

Creamy carbonara

a delicous and creamy pasta dish, best served straight from the pan!

Ingredients

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 3 rashers of Bacon
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Parsely for presentation

Steps

  1. Wait untill water has boiled and place pasta until el dente
  2. Mix 3 egg yolks, grated Parmesan and black pepper together to make the sauce mix
  3. Fry bacon untill crispy
  4. When pasta is done, place into the mixing bowl along with a spoonful of pasta water
  5. Mix until the sauce melts in with the pasta
  6. Add bacon
  7. Walllah!
